🌐 About Us

The universe won't wait.
Neither should you.

Orbit mAInd is a weekly newsletter on space and aerospace — built for readers who want to understand the industry, not just watch it from a distance. Launches, missions, scientific discoveries, and the ideas reshaping how humanity reaches beyond Earth.

We don't repackage press releases. We dig into the studies, track the companies, and surface the stories that matter — so that by the time you finish reading, you know not just what happened, but why it counts.

Our mission

We started with a simple conviction: space is too important to cover badly. The industry is moving faster than at any point since Apollo — and yet most coverage barely scratches the surface.

Orbit mAInd covers the full picture: crewed missions and robotic probes, commercial launchers and government agencies, Earth observation and deep-space science. And because the story doesn't wait, we publish daily updates across X, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok — wherever you already are.
