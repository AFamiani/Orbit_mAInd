# Instagram — Post Template

**Caption limit:** 2200 chars (algorithm shows first ~125 chars before "more" fold)
**Image:** Required — use NASA APOD, ESA image of the week, or a relevant infographic
**Links:** No clickable links in caption — always use "Link in bio"

---

## Template

```
✨ {Headline}

{Summary — 2–3 sentences, vivid and engaging. Max 300 chars. 
Use sensory language where possible. Italian preferred.}

📰 Via {Source}
🔗 Link in bio
```

## Rules

- Open with ✨ emoji + punchy headline — this is what shows in the preview
- Keep the summary vivid and visual — Instagram is an image-first platform
- **Never put a URL in the caption** — Instagram does not make them clickable; always say "Link in bio"
- Hashtags go at the very end — 15–25 hashtags is the sweet spot
- Use lowercase hashtags (more common in Italian space community)
- Image guidelines: minimum 1080×1080px, ideally 1080×1350px (portrait for feed)
- APOD images are free for non-commercial editorial use (credit NASA/ESA)
- Post at 09:00, 12:00, or 18:00 (Europe/Rome) for maximum Italian audience reach
