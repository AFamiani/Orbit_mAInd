🌐 About Us

Reporting on space,
one week at a time

Orbit mAInd is a weekly newsletter covering space and aerospace news. Every week we bring the stories that matter straight to your inbox: launches, missions, scientific discoveries, industry moves, and the policies shaping humanity's future beyond Earth.

We write for people who want to understand — not just follow — what is happening in space. No clickbait headlines, no recycled press releases: just clear, in-depth reporting on one of the most extraordinary frontiers of our time.

Our mission

Space exploration is one of humanity's most ambitious endeavours — and it deserves coverage to match. Orbit mAInd exists to close the gap between what is happening in the space industry and what most people know about it.

We cover the full spectrum: from SpaceX launches and NASA science missions to emerging players, geopolitics, and the quiet breakthroughs happening in aeronautics labs around the world. We also publish across major social channels, bringing daily updates to wherever you already are.
